About Us

Vascroft was established by two brothers – Shashi and Late Arjan Vekaria – in the early seventies. They started out as jobbing painters and general labourers working on the construction of the Barbican Towers, London.

Vision, Mission and Values

Our Vision is exciting and ambitious. We aim to target profitable growth, build business within our key markets and with our existing clients, whilst also exploring further opportunities to broaden our client base and pursue new markets. We will also build on our considerable success and expertise to provide a truly integrated one stop solution for our clients.

Corporate Responsibility

Our commitment to excellence naturally extends to providing a safe and healthy working environment for staff, partners and customers.Rigid safety training and thorough risk assessments are undertaken as part of every project; safeguarding the people for whom the business has a duty of care. Such an important aspect of the company’s activities can never be the sole responsibility of one person so everyone at Vascroft is fully aware of their responsibilities, sharing the belief that putting safety first is an essential consideration every time they go to work.

Accreditations / Certifications

ISO 9001 is the internationally recognised standard for the quality management of businesses. It applies to the processes that create and control the products and services an organisation supplies, and prescribes systematic control of activities to ensure that the needs and expectations of customers are met.

Vascroft Foundation

We created the Vascroft Foundation in July 2004 because we believe in helping others less fortunate than ourselves.The Foundation has donated, annually to education, health and vocational training projects in the UK, Africa and India. And we intend to increase our efforts and establish more projects to help more people.
